Privacy policy

How the Institute for Future Intelligence handles information you share while using Aladdin.

This policy discloses the privacy practices for the Aladdin cloud-based CAD software developed and operated by the Institute for Future Intelligence (IFI), a non-profit research organization based in Massachusetts dedicated to promoting STEM education. This policy applies solely to information collected by Aladdin for service and research purposes.

Data collection, use, and sharing

If you would like to store your Aladdin CAD files on the cloud in the long term, you will need to register with Aladdin so that it can create an account for you. Currently, Aladdin allows you to sign in using the OAuth-based Google Sign-In, which is provided by Google to simplify the registration process with a third-party app like Aladdin with a few clicks. All the user authentication steps are securely handled by Google. When you sign into Aladdin using this service from Google, Aladdin collects a unique alphanumeric string (such as P2wNcxhEdas8BN11hBstfZdDau2) used to create and access your Aladdin account. The alphanumeric ID string is used only with Aladdin and cannot be used to log into any other app or website.

With an Aladdin account, you receive benefits such as storing your CAD files on the cloud, sharing your designs with others, being notified when someone is interested in your work, and using other computational and communal facilities within Aladdin. However, if you cannot or do not want to sign in using the Google service, you can sign in anonymously to create a temporary account. In this case, no personally identifiable data such as your name and email is ever obtained or used at any point to create the account. Note that such a temporary account will be lost if you sign out from it. Every time you sign in anonymously, a new temporary account will be created, so you will not be able to access files stored in previous temporary accounts.

Your access to and control over information

If you still do not feel comfortable about the above privacy statement, you may opt out of any future relationship with Aladdin at any time. You can do the following by emailing charles@intofuture.org:

  • See what data we have about you, if any;
  • Change/correct any data we have about you;
  • Ask us to delete any data we have about you;
  • Express any concern you have about our use of your data.

As we have no other way to identify your data, please provide your alphanumeric ID string in your communication with us so we can entertain your request. Your alphanumeric ID string can be found in the "My Account Settings" page within Aladdin.

Updates

This policy may change from time to time and all updates will be posted on this page.

If you feel that we are not abiding by this privacy policy, you should contact us immediately via email: charles@intofuture.org.
