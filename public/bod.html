Board of Directors

Janice Mokros, Chair
Dr. Mokros is a Senior Research Scientist at Tumblehome. A national leader in STEM education, she has served as the Principal Investigator or Project Director of numerous federally or privately funded projects in several institutions. She holds a Ph.D. from Institute of Child Development at the University of Minnesota.
Dave Bursell
Mr. Bursell is Vice President of Business Development at MoviTHERM. Mr. Bursell manages a global team of business development and product managers to develop and execute strategic objectives for creating customer value and promote company growth. Previously, he was Vice President of Business Development and Product Management at FLIR Systems. He holds a MBA from Boston University.
Alejandra Magana
Dr. Magana is W.C. Furnas Professor in Enterprise Excellence in the Department of Computer and Information Technology at Purdue University. An internationally recognized expert in modeling and simulation for science education, she has published 60 peer-reviewed journal papers in educational research. She holds a Ph.D. in engineering education from Purdue University.
Larry Weathers
Mr. Weathers is the emeritus K-12 Director of Science and Technology of Arlington Public Schools in Massachusetts. A renowned educator with more than 40 years of teaching experience, he has received a Presidential Citation from the White House for Excellence in Science Teaching and has been inducted into the Massachusetts Teaching Hall of Fame. He holds a Master's Degree in physics from Michigan State University.
Charles Xie
Dr. Xie is the President, CEO, and Founder of IFI. A computational physicist by training, he has developed a series of scientific and engineering software used by numerous students, teachers, and researchers all over the world. Throughout his career, he has directed more than 15 research grants from the National Science Foundation and the National Institutes of Health.
