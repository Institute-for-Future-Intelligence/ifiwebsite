Privacy policy

How the Institute for Future Intelligence handles information you share while using Telelab.

← Back to Telelab

This privacy policy discloses the privacy practices for the Telelab platform developed and operated by the Institute for Future Intelligence, a non-profit research organization based in Massachusetts dedicated to promoting STEM education. It applies solely to information collected by Telelab for service and research purposes in the fields of science and education.

The policy notifies you of the following:

  • What personally identifiable information is collected from you through Telelab, how it is used, and with whom it may be shared;
  • What choices are available to you regarding the use of your data;
  • The security procedures in place to protect your information;
  • How you can correct any inaccuracies in the information.

Data collection, use, and sharing

The Institute for Future Intelligence (IFI) is the sole owner of the information collected by Telelab. When you sign in with a Google or Facebook account, they will provide your information — including your name, email, and other profile data — that is used to automatically set up your account with Telelab. With such an account, you receive benefits like participating in live experiments, accessing experimental data, and storing your experiments in the cloud. While you are using Telelab, we may also collect usage data to support our research on how people learn with Telelab, in order to improve the product.

In some cases, we may share redacted data with researchers outside IFI through inter-institutional collaboration. Under all circumstances, we will not sell or rent this information to any third party. Unless you ask us not to in your Account Settings, we may occasionally contact you via email to announce news about Telelab or changes to this privacy policy.

If you are a student participating in our research, you are additionally protected by an Institutional Review Board (IRB) currently overseen by Solutions IRB, which ensures compliance of such studies with ethical principles and other regulations.

Your access to and control over information

You may opt out of any future contact from us at any time. You can do the following by contacting us via the email address listed at the end of this page:

  • See what data we have about you, if any;
  • Change/correct any data we have about you;
  • Have us delete any data we have about you;
  • Express any concern you have about our use of your data.

Security

We take every precaution to protect your information. Your personal data is stored in a secure database. Your true identity does not show up in Telelab unless you choose to disclose it. If you need to be identified as a contributor to the Telelab community, we encourage you to use a display name as your online identity instead of your real name. The display name can be set in your Account Settings.

Updates

This policy may change from time to time and all updates will be posted on this page.

If you feel that we are not abiding by this privacy policy, please contact us immediately: charles@intofuture.org.
